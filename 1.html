Veeraraghava Swamy Temple

DISTRICT: Tiruvallur

DEITY: Sri Veeraraghav Swami

FESTIVALS: Ammavasai

FEATURES: TOWER-Vijauakoti Vimanam

TEMPLE TANK-Hritayathabhanasini

DESCRIPITION:

The temple is believed to be of significant antiquity and is believed to be initiated by the Pallavas of the late 8th century CE, with later contributions at different times from Thanjavur Nayaks. The temple is under the administration of Ahobila Matha. It has five tiered Rajagopuram (main gate). There are separate shrines for Kanakavalli, Ganesha, Alvars, and Gajalakshmi Thayar. Here, the Lord married Vasumathi the daughter of a king Dharmasena. There are also shrines to Ganesha, Gopalan, Nammalvar, Chakratalvar, Andal, Vedanta Desika, Ramanujacharyar, and Lakshmi Narasimhar.
