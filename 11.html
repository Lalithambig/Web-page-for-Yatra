SATHYATHESWARAR TEMPLE

DISTRICT: Kanchipuram

DEITY: Sathyanatheswarar (SHIVA), Bhramarambika (PARVATHI)

DESCRIPITION:

Sathyanatheswarar Temple (also called Karaivananathar Temple or Thirukalimedu temple) is a Hindu temple dedicated to Shiva, located in the town of Thirukalimedu, near Indira Theertham Kanchipuram, Kanchipuram district in Tamil Nadu, India. Shiva is worshipped as Sathyanatheswarar and his consort Parvathi as Pramarambikai.
